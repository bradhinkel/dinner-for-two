Altura none-by-design

Capitol Hill · Italian Tasting Menu · venue_format: tasting-only · volatility: daily
Source: https://alturarestaurant.com/dinnermenu · extraction: html (description only) + sample-menu PDF not parsed · captured: 2026-06-20

No a la carte menu exists by design. Single seasonal tasting menu, $175/person, ~3 hours. 'Plates will be described when served, and a menu will be provided at the end of the meal' — so there is no fixed dish list to publish or capture. Only a dated 'Sample Menu' PDF (s/MASTER-03112026.pdf) hints at structure. CANONICAL tasting-only case: composer must NOT fabricate courses; present the experience + price + pairing, route to recommendation/curation mode. Sister restaurant Carrello (across the street) is the a-la-carte option.

Tasting price: $175/person · ~180 min · sample menu PDF

The Walrus and the Carpenter partial-blocked

Ballard · Seafood / Oyster bar · venue_format: share-plate · volatility: daily
Source: https://thewalrusbar.com/menu · extraction: pdf (fetch timed out) · captured: 2026-06-20

Menu page states 'our menu changes daily.' Food and Beverage menus are dated PDFs (Walrus-Food-Menu-6.18.pdf) hosted on DigitalOcean Spaces. PDF fetch timed out at 180s — no dish data extracted. TWO compounding error cases: (a) PDF extraction needed (download + parse, or render), and (b) daily-rotating menu means any snapshot is stale within 24h. Share-plate oyster bar: store as a high-volatility row, capture a representative menu + capture date, and lean on category-level composition ('a meal to share') rather than specific dish IDs. Corkage $40/750mL.
